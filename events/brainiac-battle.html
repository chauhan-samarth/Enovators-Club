Brainiac Battle

Eligible Classes: XI-XII
No. of Participants: 02




Time Duration: 1 Hour
Topic: All aspects of computing, IT and the World of Technology


PRELIM ROUND:

● Pen-paper based prelims will be held before the on-stage Finals.
● The teams will be given 20 minutes to answer the prelims question paper.
● The top 6 teams will proceed to the Finals.


FINAL ROUND:

● There will be different rounds of Quiz for the six shortlisted teams.


JUDGEMENT CRITERIA:

● Judgement will be based on the scores obtained in the Leader Board.
