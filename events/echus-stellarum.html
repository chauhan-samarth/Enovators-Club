Echus Stellarum

Eligible Classes: IX-XII
No. of Participants: 02 - 04




● The first 16 teams to register will participate in the preliminary rounds on a first-come, first-served basis.
● Each team must designate a leader to receive event details via email and disseminate login credentials to their team members.
● Team leaders would receive all details only via the official email. Leaders must disseminate login credentials to their team for the preliminary round platform.
● Joining the event’s Discord server is mandatory for all registered participants for assistance, timely hints and overall collaborations and proper assessment.
● Any use of unfair means, unauthorised and malicious actions will lead to an immediate elimination.

PRELIM ROUND:

● Duration: 60 hours, from 5 PM on 2 August to 5 AM on 5 August.
● Platform: Access through www.enovators.in/echus-stellarum using provided login credentials.
● Challenges: Participants must submit accurate solutions to progress.
● Leaderboard: Updated every 8 hours at www.enovators.in/echus-stellarum/leaderboard.
● Results: Officially announced on 5 August at 5 PM on www.enovators.in/result.
● Recommendations: Participants may use the Internet, ciphers, Google Earth/Maps, etc.
● Discord: Mandatory for guidance and assistance. Contact helpline via Discord moderators or WhatsApp at +91 9897755401 for login issues or reports of misconduct.
● Extended Rules & Guidelines: Available at www.enovators.in/echus-stellarum/guidelines


FINAL ROUND:

● The top 6 teams from the preliminary round will advance.
● Teams must bring their own devices and ensure a stable internet connection.
● Teams may utilize preliminary references to solve the final puzzles.
● Discord usage is forbidden during this round.
● The final result will be definitive and non-negotiable.
