Echus Stellarum

Eligible Classes: IX-XII
No. of Participants: 02 - 04




● The first 16 teams would be admitted for the preliminaries as a first come first service entry policy.
● Each team must designate a team leader acting as the primary source of communication via email.
● Team leaders would receive all details only via the official email. Leaders must disseminate login credentials to their team for the preliminary round platform.
● Participants are encouraged to join the event’s Discord server for assistance, inquiries, and collaboration and proper assessment. The link will be emailed to registered participants.
● Unauthorized behavior, including cheats and third-party cracking attempts, leads to immediate disqualification.

PRELIM ROUND:

● Duration: 60 hours, from 5 PM on 2 August to 5 AM on 5 August.
● Platform: www.enovators.in/echus-stellarum.
● Access: Use provided login credentials to login on the preliminary round platform.
● Challenges: Accurate solution submissions required for progression.
● Leaderboard: Updated every 8 hours on www.enovators.in/echus-stellarum/leaderboard.
● Results: Announced at www.enovators.in/result on 5 August at 5 PM.
● Recommendations: Use of the Internet, Ciphers, Google Earth/Maps, etc. is allowed.
● Discord: Compulsory for guidance and assistance. Helpline available via Discord moderators or WhatsApp on +91 9897755401 in case of login issues or reports of misconduct.
● Extended Rules- www.enovators.in/echus-stellarum/guidelines


FINAL ROUND:

● The top 6 teams from the preliminary round will advance.
● Teams may use preliminary references to solve the final puzzles.
● The usage of Discord is forbidden for this round.
● The final result would be conclusive and binding.
