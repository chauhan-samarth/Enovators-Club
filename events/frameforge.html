FrameForge

Eligible Classes: IX-XII
No. of Participants: 02




Topic: Youth Activism and Social Changes
Time Duration of Video: 3-4 Minutes
Software: Any video editing software

PRELIM ROUND:

● Participants are required to create a video based on the given topic, with a duration of 3 to 4 minutes.
● The use of templates is prohibited.
● The video should be uploaded to the participant's YouTube channel as an unlisted video. The title should be the name of the film, and the description must include the name of the school, the topic of the video, and the names and classes of the participants.
● The YouTube URL must be submitted by 31st July 2024 at 5:00 PM on www.enovators.in/submit/frameforge.


FINAL ROUND:

● The top 6 shortlisted teams will present their work before the judges on 9th August 2024 by 5:00 PM.
● Students are required to bring their videos on USB drives.



JUDGEMENT CRITERIA:

● Editing
● Video Quality
● Uniqueness of the Project
