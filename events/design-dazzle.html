DESIGN DAZZLE

Eligible Classes: II-III
No. of Participants: 01




Topic: 1. Helping Others (For the Prelims Round)
2. To be given on the spot (For the Final Round)
Time Duration: 1 Hour
Software: MS Paint
Max File Size: 10MB

PRELIM ROUND:

● Participant need to draw a picture using MS Paint based on the given Topic.
● Participant need to upload the (Png/Jpeg) file on www.enovators.in/submit/design-dazzle latest by 31st July 2024 by 5:00 PM.
● Plagiarism of any kind is unacceptable and would lead to disqualification.


FINAL ROUND:

● 8 Shortlisted entries will compete in the final round and participant need to draw another picture on the topic which will be given on the spot-on 09th Aug 2024.



JUDGEMENT CRITERIA:

● Relevance to Topic
● Creativity and Color Composition
● Use of tools
