Game Blitz

Eligible Classes: IX-XII
No. of Participants: 04




Game: Call of Duty Mobile (COD)
● Participants must have COD with all modes installed and a good internet connection on their respective devices.
● All players will be individually monitored. Any team using unfair means will be disqualified.
● The host school will not be responsible for any crippling software, lag or high ping.
● Please Keep in mind that spaces are limited, and registrations will be processed on a first-come, first-served basis.
● The first 16 registered schools will be selected for the prelims round.
● The match results will be disclosed on www.enovators.in/result


PRELIM ROUND:

Game Mode: Search and Destroy
● All the registered teams will come face-to-face in an intense game on Saturday, 03rd August 2024 at 9:00 AM.
● The top 8 teams will qualify for the next round.


FINAL ROUND:

● The 8 teams which qualify will now go on a 2 vs 2 format in the Gunfight mode.
● This will continue until 2 teams are left.
● The Final Match will be played in Domination Mode.
