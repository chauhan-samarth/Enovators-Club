Artistic Canvas

Eligible Classes: I-II

No. of Participants: 02

The Event will be held in one round.




RULES AND REGULATIONS

● Topic: Unleash Your Imagination: Design Your Own Magical Pet!

● Time Duration: 1 Hour

● Software: Tux Paint

● We invite participants to embark on a captivating artistic journey where they can let their imaginations run wild and create their very own magical pet.

● Participants must use Tux Paint software to create their magical pet artwork The artwork should revolve around the theme of a magical pet, allowing students to explore their imagination and creativity.

● Judge's decision will be final.



Judgment Criteria

● Creativity and Imagination

● Artistic Expression

● Originality

● Theme Interpretation

● Visual Appeal
