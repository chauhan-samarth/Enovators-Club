WORDSMITH

Time Duration: 1 Hour
Eligible Class: V
No. of Participants: 01




● Topic: Virtual Friends Vs Real Friends
● Time Duration: 1 Hour
● Software: MS Word
● Max File Size: 10MB

PRELIM ROUND:

● Participants need to design an infographic image based on the given topic.
● Image should be designed to quickly and comprehensively communicate key facts using stats, graphs and more data representing options. The final image should be of dimensions 1000 X 1200 pixel (10'-12.5') of format Png, Jpg or pdf.
● Participants need to upload their image file on www.enovators.in/submit/wordsmith latest by 05th October 2023 by 5:00 PM.
● Plagiarism of any kind is unacceptable and would lead to disqualification.


FINAL ROUND:

● 6 shortlisted teams will present their infographic image in front of the judges on 12th October 2023. Students are requested to bring the image in their USB drives.
● Judge's decision will be final and binding.



JUDGEMENT CRITERIA:

● Relevance to Topic
● Clarity and Informative
● Creativity
● Innovation and Overall appeal
