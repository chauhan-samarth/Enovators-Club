GENERAL GUIDELINES

● Schools must register online at www.enovators.in latest by Saturday, September 30, 2023.

● Participants must report at the registration desk latest by 8:00 a.m. on the day of the event.

● Any changes, must be notified beforehand at support@enovators.in or at the registration desk.

● Preliminary rounds in some events will be based on online submission. Teachers In Charge are requested to submit student’s work on www.enovators.in/submit. The entry should be submitted on the respective event forms latest by Thursday, October 05th, 2023 by 5:00 PM.

● Results of the preliminary rounds will be declared on www.enovators.in/result on Saturday, October 07th, 2023 by 5:00 PM.

● No Student can participate in more than one event.

● Only one team is allowed per school in a particular event.

● For scoring first position in an event school will be awarded with 100 points, for second position 80 points and for third position 50 points. The school with maximum points will be awarded the Winning Trophy.

● Participants should be in proper school uniform and must carry their School-ID card on the day of event.

● Please ensure that a teacher accompanies the participants.

● Participants are requested to take care of their devices and belongings. The host school will not be responsible for any loss or damage to the devices.

● Judges’ decision in all the events will be final and binding



For any queries, you may write to us at support@enovators.in with the name of
the event mentioned as the subject or contact us
